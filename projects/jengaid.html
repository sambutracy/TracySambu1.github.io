JengaID — Decentralized Identity Platform

What it is: Web3 identity prototype for creating and managing digital credentials with wallet-native user flows.

Tech stack: Next.js, TypeScript, Privy, Wagmi, Web3Modal, Hardhat, Web3.Storage, Tailwind CSS.

My contribution: Built the app foundation, integrated auth/wallet flows, and connected decentralized storage workflows.

Potential impact: Demonstrates portable identity architecture for trust-based hiring and opportunity discovery ecosystems.
