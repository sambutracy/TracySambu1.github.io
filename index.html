About

I am a builder operating across Web2, Web3, AI, and geospatial systems. My career did not follow a straight line—and that journey shaped how I solve problems: pragmatically, creatively, and with empathy for users and teams.

  • Started in geospatial engineering and learned to think in systems.
  • Pivoted into software through ALX, GitHub, and hands-on shipping.
  • Joined hackathons and turned pressure into rapid experimentation.
  • Balanced community work, freelancing, and product building.
  • Repeated the build-feedback-improve loop until it became my operating system.

Result: A developer who can build, explain, support, and ship.
